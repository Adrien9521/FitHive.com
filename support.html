FAQ's
What is FitHive?
   FitHive is more than just a gym; it's a community-driven wellness hub designed to empower individuals on their fitness journey. At FitHive, we believe in fostering a supportive environment where members can achieve their fitness goals, whether they're beginners or seasoned athletes.
How do I book a session?
   In order to book a session, you can follow these simple steps:
  1. Vist out Website
  2. Check available dates and trainers
  3. Call or schedule through a message
  4. Come train with us!
What sessions do you offer?
   We offer a variety of sessions, including group fitness classes, personal training sessions, and specialty workshops tailored to various fitness levels and goals.
Are there safety measures?
   Your safety is our top priority. We adhere to strict safety protocols, including regular equipment sanitization, social distancing measures, and limited class sizes to ensure a safe and comfortable environment for all participants.
What about my limitations?
   Our trainers are skilled at designing customized workout plans tailored to individual goals, fitness levels, and any specific limitations or injuries you may have. During your initial consultation, we'll work together to create a plan that's right for you.
